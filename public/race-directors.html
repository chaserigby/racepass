What is Racepass all about? How does it work?
Our mission is to help runners to run more races, and in turn for your races to have more runners. Racepass subscribers buy a certain amount of race credits and give us consent to register them for their desired races. Racepass then registers the runner directly with your race always using your preferred channels (e.g. your race management platform or provider) driving 100% of the retail value to you. Our goal is to build a product that is good for runners, and good for race directors.
How does Racepass partner with races?
Racepass integrates directly and indirectly with races. If you see your race listed and we do not have a direct relationship, Racepass has worked with your race management platform or is listing it through sourced events databases from such organizations as USATF, etc. When a runner expresses interest in running your race, Racepass then signs the runner up through your preferred channels.
I don't see my race. How do I get it listed?
If your race is not listed (and you want it to be) contact our partnerships team, partners@racepass.com for inclusion and feel free to ask about other partnership opportunities.
How can I get more registrants for my race?
Racepass has direct access to thousands of active runners and can help promote your race and increase registrants. Email our partnerships team, partners@racepass.com or fill out an inquiry form here.
Are there advertising opportunities with Racepass?
Of course! There are multiple opportunities to promote relevant products, brands and events on our platform. For advertising inquiries contact partners@racepass.com
How can Racepass help me find sponsorships?
That's what we are working on next. Through building the largest network of races, we can facilitate corporate sponsorship opportunities that will help finance race operations. To find out more email our partnerships team, partners@racepass.com
I need help organizing my race, can Racepass help?
While Racepass does not supply race management or timing solutions, we can help promote and increase exposure to your race, helping it be a success. Please contact our partnerships team, partners@racepass.com, for further information.

If you have questions or concerns, we always advise you check with your own insurance policies and legal counsel and if they have questions they can contact legal@racepass.com

Any questions running through your mind?
What is Racepass?
Racepass is a subscription-based annual pass that helps runners run as many races as they can muster.
Where can I use Racepass?
Racepass can be used at over 5,000 race events across the nation and more are being added everyday. For complete race listings please see racepass.com/map.
How many races can I run?
Each Racepass plan has its own limitations. Please see our pricing page for complete plan details.
When does Racepass membership begin?
You can begin signing up for races immediately after completing your purchase.
More FAQs
Does my Racepass auto-renew?
Racepass will allow you to auto-renew your membership. If you choose to not auto-renew, you can opt out through your subscription settings in your profile.
What are the benefits of Racepass?
Racepass eliminates the administrative headache of finding, registering and recruiting friends for races. It also allows you to run as many races as you can, tracking your times to help you train and run faster.
Where can I use Racepass?
Racepass can be used at over 5,000 race events across the nation and more are being added everyday.
How many races can I run?
Each Racepass plan is different. Check out our pricing tiers for complete plan details.
Is there a cancellation policy?
Flexibility is a good thing. You can cancel your Racepass at anytime! To cancel your Racepass, head to your subscription settings in your profile.
